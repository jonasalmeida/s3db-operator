Propagation of S3DB operator states

The propagation of S3DB operator states is resolved by three individual functions - merge, migrate and percolate.

This browser-based application dissects the functioning of all three, with the alphabetic indexes and propagation topologies chosen by the user. All three functions are javascript implementations of the original m-code versionm, link provided.

1. Merge

An operator state describes the relationship between a user and an S3DB entity. Its alphabetic order indicates precedence, and its lower or upper case will indicate, respectively, recessive or dominant nature. See equation 2 for calculation.
